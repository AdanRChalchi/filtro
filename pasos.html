Pasos

1. Watch on YouTube "How to make a water filter".

2. Get together to build a homemade water filter.

3. Get the next materials to create it:

  • 1 plastic container with cylinder shape and funnel termination, approximately 1/2m height, in another case, an empty bottle
  • 1Kg fine sand
  • 1kg gravel
  • 1kg of small rocks
  • 1kg of medium rocks
  • 1/2 kg of activated charcoal

4. The part of the container with the funnel termination is put down. On this, it will have little perforations to allow the water to come out. Later, wash it with water and soap When it's clean the container, put it in a base that allows to keep standing and be able to the water flows.

5. Fill the container with the mentioned materials. The layers must measure as indicated.
  • Small rocks 10cm
  • Medium rocks 15cm
  • Gravel 3cm
  • Charcoal 3cm
  • Gravel 2cm
  • Fine sand 5cm
  • Gravel 5cm
  • Small rocks 10cm
6. When the filter is ready, test it.
